About

IUCEE EWB MCE

The IUCEE EWB MCE Student Chapter is a dynamic, student-run organisation that unites driven individuals to solve practical problems via service and innovation. In collaboration with Engineers Without Borders (EWB), our chapter equips students with real-world skills and a humanitarian perspective by going beyond the conventional curriculum. One sustainable solution at a time, we're improving the world outside of the classroom. Students can develop into socially conscious engineers through a variety of activities, such as interactive workshops on cutting-edge technologies, community-based projects, and national events like AAVISHKAR MANTHAN. Our goal is to establish an environment where students can work together, be creative, and actually change society.

Know More

Aavishkar Manthan

Aavishkar Manthan is a prestigious national-level inspire innovation and awareness among students through the lens of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). With a focus on real-world impact, this event invites participants from across the country to explore ideas that contribute to building a more sustainable and equitable future. The three flagship events of Aavishkar Manthan are Ideathon, Elocution and Walkathon.
